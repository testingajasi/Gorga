How to start Sustainable Living?

There a simple way to start Sustainable Living.

Eating a Plant-Based Diet Composting Reducing Single-Use Plastic

You can start cutting down on your plastic waste in a few simple steps: Use reusable bags when you shop, ditch single-use water bottles, bags and straws, and avoid products made from or packaged in plastic whenever possible (e.g., select unwrapped produce at the grocery store).

Instead of sending food scraps to landfill, start composting. Composting is a controlled, aerobic (oxygen-required) process that converts organic materials into a nutrient-rich, biologically-stable soil amendment or mulch through natural decomposition.
